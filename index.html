Kazi Nazrul Islam

The National Poet of Bangaladesh

Rebel poet

Kazi Nazrul Islam was a Bengali poet,,revolutionary and who is best known for poineering works of Bengali .He is probably known as the Bidrodhi Kobi -Rebel Poet- as many of his works showcase an intense rebellion against oppression of human through slavery,hatred and tradition.


Awesome Writer

He was a voice of Islam proclaiming universal values, peace, freedom, justice, harmony and cooperation. His writings were based on the theme of love, freedom and revolution. He was a writer of short stories, novels and essays but was best known for his poetry.
